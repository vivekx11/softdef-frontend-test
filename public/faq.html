Frequently Asked Questions...

How do I care for my plant when it arrives?expand_more

Unbox gently and place in indirect light for 1-2 days to acclimate. Water lightly after 24 hours. Don't fertilize for 2 weeks. Check our Care Guides for species-specific advice.

Do you ship internationally?expand_more

Currently we ship within the continental US. International shipping is coming soon! Sign up for our newsletter to be notified.

What if my plant arrives damaged?expand_more

We offer a 30-day plant health guarantee. If your plant arrives damaged, email us a photo at returns@verdant.com and we'll send a free replacement within 48 hours.

Are your plants pet-safe?expand_more

We label pet-friendly plants clearly. Check the product badge for "Pet Friendly" tags. When in doubt, we recommend keeping all plants out of reach of curious pets.

Do you offer wholesale pricing?expand_more

Yes! We partner with offices, hotels, and interior designers. Contact us for wholesale pricing starting at 20+ units.

What's your packaging like?expand_more

100% plastic-free! We use recycled cardboard, corn-starch packing peanuts, and paper-based tape. The inner sleeve is compostable and keeps your plant secure during transit.
