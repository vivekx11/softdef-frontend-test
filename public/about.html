About Our Farm

Founded in 2019, Verdant Botanical began as a small passion project in a backyard greenhouse. Today, we're one of the most trusted online plant retailers, serving over 50,000 plant parents across the country.

Every plant in our collection is hand-reared from seed or cutting in our solar-powered greenhouse facility. We believe plants aren't products—they're living companions that deserve the same care and attention you'd give any member of your family.
