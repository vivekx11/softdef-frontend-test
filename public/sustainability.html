Our Sustainability Promise

Every plant we grow, package, and deliver follows our commitment to leaving the earth greener than we found it.

eco

Zero-Waste Farming

Our greenhouse uses 100% renewable energy. Plant waste is composted and returned to the soil. We use rainwater collection systems for irrigation.

inventory_2

Plastic-Free Packaging

Every order ships in biodegradable packaging made from recycled cardboard and corn-starch peanuts. Even our tape is paper-based.

local_shipping

Carbon-Neutral Delivery

We offset 100% of our shipping emissions through verified reforestation projects. Local deliveries are made by electric vehicle.

volunteer_activism

Community Impact

5% of every sale goes to urban greening initiatives. We've planted over 10,000 trees in underserved communities since 2020.
